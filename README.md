# Olist E-Commerce Business Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end E-Commerce Business Analysis using the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, and interactive dashboard development in Power BI.

The project demonstrates the complete Data Analyst workflow from data preparation to business reporting.

## Key Business Insights

* Total Revenue exceeded 16 Million.
* Credit Card payments contributed more than 78% of total revenue.
* Delivered orders accounted for approximately 97% of all orders.
* Average customer review score remained above 4.0.
* São Paulo generated the highest revenue among all states.
* Beauty, Sports & Leisure, and Bed & Bath categories were among the top-performing product categories.
* Average delivery time was approximately 12.5 days.
